## Enterprise IAM Experience

### [Technical Implementation Lead: Enterprise MFA Implementation](./Enterprise-MFA-Implementation/)

Led the technical rollout and governance of an enterprise **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** platform within a business environment focused on secure access and operational control.

#### Key Contributions
- Configured authentication controls, security policies, and deployment standards to support secure user access.
- Authored technical SOPs, troubleshooting frameworks, and self-service guidance adopted as the departmental standard.
- Embedded MFA processes into the **Joiner-Mover-Leaver (JML)** lifecycle to strengthen access governance.
- Supported the controlled rollout of MFA across operational workflows and business processes.
- Contributed to the standardisation of identity-related support and governance procedures.

#### Core IAM Themes
- Authentication engineering
- Access governance
- Identity lifecycle management
- Operational standardisation
- Secure user provisioning and deprovisioning
- Enterprise delivery and control alignment
